About Hashman Infused

Hashman Infused is a startup business that sells and creates medical cannabis goods. Often advertised together, their counterpart business is Waxman Concentrates. As a growing business, they're discovering the best ways to reach their target audiences through e-mail campaigns and social media. Current projects with this business include developing marketing material for their upcoming campaigns for: e-mail, fliers, table tents, Instagram, etc. I've also shot some of their product photography as they re-new their product line.
