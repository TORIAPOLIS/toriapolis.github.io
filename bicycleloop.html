Davis Bicycle Loop

2015

Link to process book: HERE

Project | Redevelop a visual identification system and wayifinding signage for the Davis Bicycle Loop.

The Davis Bicycle Loop | It is a 12 mile route around the city of Davis created as a pleasant and scenic pathway for walking, jogging, and bicycling.

Logo | The unifying element in rebranding the Davis Bicycle Loop to "The Loop" is the ferris wheel/bike tire like logo. Each circle symbolizes a vital component of Davis connected to a central hub of community. The intention is to unify the bike rider with the ease and joy of their journey.
