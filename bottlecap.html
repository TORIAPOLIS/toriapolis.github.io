Bottlecap Collecting

Category

User Interface

What kind of mobile app would bottle collectors want?

An exercise on prototyping a mobile application for bottlecap enthusiasts.

Three are three main features: Store, Collection, and News. Navigation is housed in a menu bar at the top, where the user can tap on the menu items or swipe left or right from page to page. As this was an assignment with a deadline, it was an initial iteration. If I were to design it again, I would make the news page less compact. I would give each article more padding around the content and add more content to read without having to go into the link beforehand.
