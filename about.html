My Story

As I graduate college in a couple months, I still haven't figured out whether I like to write in the first or third person more. I studied at UC Davis for nearly five years in a variety of majors that when combined all together turned into this super neat-o amalgamation of a major called DESIGN. I like creative projects and I love helping people. So when every other major was a wealth of knowledge, I wanted to pull it all together to do something meaningful for both me and others.

"Research, Ideate, Design!"

Victoria is interested in the intersectionality of design and the realms of: education, health, and social change. She believes design is a powerful solution to promoting awareness and education of topics like invisible disabilities, self-care, and sustainable living. A majority of her time is spent brainstorming and researching in order to design the best experiences for the user. She enjoys designing both digital and environmental experiences.
