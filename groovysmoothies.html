Groovy Smoothies

Category

Game Design, UI/UX

Play Groovie Smoothies
Client

The client is Sacramento's Crocker Art Museum. They have an education program for kids called the Art Ark, " a mobile art education center designed for students in pre-K through 8th grades" teaching them topics related to art.

The Project

The client sought a digital game to teach basic color theory to kids in elementary school. Groovy Smoothies is a color matching and mixing game self-coded in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. By recognizing the color and being able to read short instructions, the user has to match that color with a fruit of the same color and drop the fruit into the smoothie maker. When the user has filled up the cup, the colors placed into the cup will blend together to create a new color.

Storyboard Points
  • 9 year old Kelvin enters the Art Ark.
  • He faces a computer screen and is prompted to start a new game.
  • He reads the directions at the top of the screen that tells him how many of a certain colored fruit/vegetable to put into the smoothie cup.
  • He guesses incorrectly and the screen alerts him of his error.
  • Kelvin tries again and the food item he picks gets added to the smoothie.
  • Kelvin fills the cup and presses a button to blend the smoothie of colors.
