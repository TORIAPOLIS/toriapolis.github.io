Gubi

2016

Link to full process manual: HERE

Developing a brand identity for a gummy vitamin product called Gubi.

The target audience encompasses young, trendy, environmentally conscious females who may have children. There are both adult and children versions of the supplement so it's a full range service with sustainable packaging.

A challenge with this design is differentiating it from its competitors of mass market gummy vitamin supplements and finding the right niche.
