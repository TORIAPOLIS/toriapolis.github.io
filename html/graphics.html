ABOUT

These are assorted graphics I've made in my spare time for various purposes.


This was the landing page you'd see when my site was under construction. The girl is one of many attempts to brand myself. I think it may have potential because my strength in illustration is in depicting people. It is a good representation of what I enjoy doing, but I do not feel like it's the best representation of my brand identity yet.


I played with the idea of a cube representing my different strengths as another way to brand myself. I think I need to do more research on branding. I did different color variations, but I feel that the first one with a teal background has the best contrast.


I used to illustrate my daily outfits with marker and pencil. After learning Illustrator, I wanted to continue journaling my wardrobe with a uniform doll I made in Illustrator. Here I've made several different hairstyles. I plant to change the body proportions so that all the dolls look unified even with the different outfits.
