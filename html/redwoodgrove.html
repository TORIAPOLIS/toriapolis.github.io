ABOUT THE PROJECT

The objective of this project was to buid an informative website about an aspect of the UC Davis campus. I originally chose the entire UC Davis Arboretum. After doing some initial research and sketches, I decided to focus on the Redwood Grove. The live version can be seen at this link: Explore the Redwood Grove.



My preliminary sketches were for the landing page. I played with the idea of using vertical navigation along with a single page vertical scroll website to follow the height of a Redwood Tree. I also thought I wanted to feature an interactive map of the entire arboretum, where you can click points on the map to check out different features of the map.



After going out into the Redwood Grove for photos, I realized I wasn't able to quite capture the single page site design I wanted to do because of photography limitations. So under my time constraints, I went with a simple Enter page, with four other pages to navigate afterwards.



These are some of the pages of the site. The focus is on the page descriptions and the photograph in the background. I would have liked to illustrate the whole website instead, but I think when encouraging people to visit a place, using real photographs is encouraging and eases them into going.
