ABOUT THE PROJECT

My employer, the UC Davis Center For Plant Diversity AKA the Herbarium, has asked me to distribute media to reach out to students and other members of the Davis Community. The Herbarium has various events throughout the year and mainly publicizes within the Plant Sciences Department. It's my responsibility to optimize their online presence. Eventually I will help them redesign their website.


The Biodiversity Museum Day is an event targeted towards families to encourage children to be involved in the sciences. On this day, 6 collections and museums open up at UC Davis and there are activities and tours to participate in. I wanted the poster to be kid-friendly and exciting with all the vibrant colors. I chose not incorporate the map of the locations on this poster, and have left it as a separate resource that can be printed and used on the day of the event.



The Winter Botanical Tea is an annual event hosted by the Herbarium celebrating a different plant collection each year. I designed this with the intention of printing it as an invitation card to the friends of the Herbarium. In this iteration, I used the serifs font for an elegant look to match a mature audience that would be attending. The contrast with the bold sans serif "Winter Botanical Tea" is done for clarity.



This Faceboook flyer I made for the Herbarium Facebook page is one of the first things I made outside of class after taking my first graphic design class. It acts as a reminder that we have an event coming up, so any further details for the event is posted in the Facebook description instead of directly on the flier. If I had a chance to re-do this flier, I would not overlay "Volunteer Sunday Afternoons" over the background photo as it is difficult to read.
