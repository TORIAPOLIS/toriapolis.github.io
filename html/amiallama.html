Am I a llama?

Interactive - Web

ABOUT THE PROJECT

The Aerospace Museum of California has a program where they hope to encourage young kids about the ages of 6 to 12 to get involved with STEM topics. I was asked to create a website that would engage this target audience in a STEM related topic. There was also the stipulation to make it work on an iPhone or tablet. The topic I chose? How to differentiate llamas alpacas. Check out the site here: Am I a Llama?


The landing page begins with Jimmy, the alpaca. Except Jimmy doesn't know what he is. The next few pages explore what an alpaca and llama are and how they are different. At the end, we are brought back to jimmy and a one question quiz for the user to respond to.



I intentionally made the website brief because I wanted to maintain interest in the kids without boring them. If I had more time I would animate illustrations, but with time constraints, I added hover effects to the buttons so that there was some sort of movement on each page.
