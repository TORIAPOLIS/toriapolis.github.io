What is Foodful.ly

Foodful.ly is a mobile application that tracks your food purchases and notifies you when your food is about to spoil. According to nrdc.org, the United States wastes up to 40% of its food. Foodful.ly exists to decrease that percentage by encouraging users to keep track of what they buy and suggesting recipes based on the items you've got in your fridge.

The app has three major features: Fridge, Stove, and Add. Within the Fridge, you can see all the items you own and when they will expire. The Stove lists recipes generated using the items based on what you have in your fridge to encourage you to use up what you have before you go out and buy more groceries. The Add feature allows you to manually add food items into your fridge as well as allowing you to connect to certain grocery store accounts you may have.
